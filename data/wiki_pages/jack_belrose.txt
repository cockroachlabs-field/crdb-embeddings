"John S. (Jack) Belrose" (born 24 November 1926), is a Canadians|Canadian radio scientist. He was born in the small town of Warner, Alberta. He attended the University of Cambridge, where he was awarded a PhD in 1958. He has worked for the Defence Research Telecommunications Establishment (DRTE) for 33 years.
