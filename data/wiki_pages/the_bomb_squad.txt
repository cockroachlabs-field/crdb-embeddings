   "The Bomb Squad" were an American hip hop production team known for its work with Hip hop music|hip hop group Public Enemy. The Bomb Squad is noted for its dense, distinct, innovative  production style, often utilizing dozens of samples on just one track. They are also known for incorporating harsh, Atonality|atonal sounds and samples into their productions.
