"Higashi-ku" (東区) is the name of several Wards of Japan|wards located in various cities in Japan.  The name literally translated to "Eastern District", and hence it is a very common ward name. * Higashi-ku, Sapporo, Hokkaido Prefercture * Higashi-ku, Niigata, Niigata Prefecture * Higashi-ku, Hamamatsu, Shizuoka Prefecture * Higashi-ku, Nagoya, Aichi Prefecture * Higashi-ku, Sakai, Osaka Prefecture * Higashi-ku, Okayama, Okayama Prefecture * Higashi-ku, Hiroshima, Hiroshima Prefecture * Higashi-ku, Fukuoka, Fukuoka Prefecture * Higashi-ku, Kumamoto, Kumamoto Prefecture * Higashi-ku of Osaka merged with Minami-ku and is now part of Chūō-ku, Osaka|Chūō-ku
