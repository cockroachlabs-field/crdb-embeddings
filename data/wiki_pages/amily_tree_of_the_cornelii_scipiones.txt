This is the family tree of the Cornelia gens|Cornelii Scipiones — a prominent family of the Roman Republic — who were allied with the Sempronia gens|Sempronii Gracchi, Aemilia gens|Aemilii Paulli, and Caecilii Metelli, whose members are also shown. Only magistracies attested with certainty in Thomas Robert Shannon Broughton|Broughton's "Magistrates of the Roman Republic" have been mentioned. The dotted lines show adoptions from natural fathers.
