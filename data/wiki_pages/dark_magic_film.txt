""Dark Magic"" (1939) is a short film|short comedy film produced by Robert Benchley for Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer. Benchley appears as Joe Doakes, who goes to a magic shop to see a magician perform magic tricks. Sharp-eyed viewers will recognize "Mr. Calypso", the magic demonstrator, as none other than sleight-of-hand master John Scarne. ==See also== *List of Robert Benchley collections and film appearances ==References== * Nathaniel Benchley, "Robert Benchley, a biography".
