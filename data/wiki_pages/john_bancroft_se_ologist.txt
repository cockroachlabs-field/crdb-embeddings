  Dr "John H.J. Bancroft" (born 1936) is a physician who was Director of The Kinsey Institute for Research in Sex, Gender, and Reproduction at Indiana University Bloomington|Indiana University from 1995 to 2004. He was a Clinical Professor of Psychiatry at Indiana University School of Medicine. Bancroft received his Bachelor of Arts|B.A. in 1960 and his Doctor of Medicine|M.D. in 1970 from the University of Cambridge|Cambridge University. Bancroft was succeeded as Director of the Kinsey Institute in 2004 by Julia Heiman.
