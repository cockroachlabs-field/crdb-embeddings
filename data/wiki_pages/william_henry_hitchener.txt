   "William Henry Hitchener" was a British author. He had two books published in 1813, "St. Leonard's Forest, Vol. 1.2" (London Chapple 1813 ) and "The Tower of Ravenwold, Vol. 1.2" (London Chapple 1813 ). Both books are travel books.
