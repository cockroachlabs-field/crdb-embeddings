
This 1988 Mercedes-Benz 560SL is finished in white over blue leather and
powered by a 5.5-liter V8 mated to a four-speed automatic transmission.
The car has been registered in Pennsylvania since 1989 and was acquired by
the selling dealer in March 2020. Equipment includes a replacement dark
blue convertible top, cruise control, automatic climate control, and an
aftermarket Alpine CD player. This R107 shows 88k miles and is offered by
the selling dealer with a removable hardtop, manufacturer's literature,
service records, a clean Carfax report, and a clean Pennsylvania title.
