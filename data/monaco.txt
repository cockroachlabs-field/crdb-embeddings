A legendary destination on the Riviera between France and Italy, Monaco and its remarkable heritage have been charming visitors since the Belle Epoque. From palaces to luxury hotels, its wonderful locations immerse us in the art of living like few other places. But the Principality has lots of other things to offer too. Its enchanting coastline is all breathtaking panoramas, idyllic beaches and pools, water sports and more. Succumb to la dolce vita in your relaxing haven on the shores of the Mediterranean and punctuate your stay with exciting and festive activities.
